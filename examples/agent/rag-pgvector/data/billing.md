# Billing

You can update the billing email and payment method on your account
under Settings > Billing. Changes take effect on the next billing
cycle; in-flight invoices are not retroactively re-issued.

Invoices are generated on the first of each month and emailed to
the billing email on file. PDF copies of every past invoice live
under Settings > Billing > Invoices.

Disputed charges should be raised with support within 60 days of
the invoice date so we can investigate before the payment
processor's chargeback window closes.
