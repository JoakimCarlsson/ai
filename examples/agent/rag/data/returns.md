# Return policy

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund,
provided they are unused and in the original packaging. Final-sale
and personalised items are exempt.

To start a return, log in to your account and visit the Orders page,
then click "Request return" next to the order in question. A prepaid
shipping label is emailed within one business day.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7
business days after the warehouse confirms receipt of the return.
