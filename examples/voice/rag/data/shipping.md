# Shipping

Standard shipping (free on orders over $50) takes 3 to 5 business
days domestically and 7 to 14 business days internationally.

Express shipping ($14.95 flat rate, $24.95 international) ships the
same business day for orders placed before 2pm Pacific time and
arrives the next business day domestically, 2 to 4 business days
internationally.

Tracking links are emailed within an hour of the package leaving the
warehouse.
